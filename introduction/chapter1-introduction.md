# Chapter 1: Introduction to Artificial Intelligence

## What is Artificial Intelligence?
Artificial Intelligence (AI) refers to computer systems that can perform tasks that normally require human intelligence. These tasks include learning, problem-solving, decision-making, and understanding language.

## Brief History of AI
AI began as a field of study in the 1950s. Over time, it has evolved due to advances in computing power, data availability, and algorithms.

## Where We See AI Today
AI is used in many areas of everyday life:
- Smartphones (voice assistants like Siri)
- Streaming platforms (movie recommendations)
- Banking (fraud detection)
- Healthcare (diagnosis support)

## Why AI is Important
AI helps:
- Improve efficiency
- Automate repetitive tasks
- Support better decision-making
