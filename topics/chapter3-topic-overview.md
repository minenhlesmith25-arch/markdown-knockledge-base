# Chapter 3: Overview of Artificial Intelligence

## Types of AI

### 1. Narrow AI (Weak AI)
- Designed to perform a specific task
- Examples:
  - Chatbots
  - Recommendation systems

### 2. General AI (Strong AI)
- Can perform any intellectual task like a human
- Still theoretical and not yet achieved

---

## Common Applications of AI
- Virtual assistants (e.g., Alexa, Siri)
- Image recognition (facial recognition systems)
- Self-driving cars
- Online recommendations (Netflix, YouTube)
